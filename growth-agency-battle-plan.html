The US LLC - two Pakistan-based founders

⚠️ Landmine caught by the research: Mercury AND Relay both BAN Pakistan-resident beneficial owners in 2026. Every generic “just use Mercury” guide is wrong for you. Working banking stack = Wise Business (primary, links to Stripe) + Payoneer (backup + marketplace payouts).
Entity

Wyoming LLC - $100 filing, $60/yr, no state income tax, member privacy, strong bank name-recognition, 1–3 day processing.

Not Delaware ($350/yr, only for VC/C-Corp). Not New Mexico (weaker bank recognition).

  • Form via Northwest Registered Agent (~$139 total) - best privacy, uses its own address on public filing
  • Operating agreement essential (multi-member, 50/50) - template + optional one-time attorney review
  • EIN without SSN - Form SS-4, line 7b = “Foreign”, fax it (~4–7 business days - the critical-path bottleneck)
Money in
  • Wise Business (primary) - accepts Pakistani-passport US-LLC owners, real US ACH details. Enhanced KYC on Pakistan founders → have a live website + a client invoice/contract + virtual mailbox ready before applying.
  • Payoneer (backup + marketplace payouts). Note: can’t be a Stripe payout destination.
  • Stripe sits on top of Wise - the whole reason to form the LLC (Stripe doesn’t operate in Pakistan).
  • PayPal Business - secondary, mixed reliability.

Order of ops: LLC → EIN → Wise → Stripe.

⚖️ Must go to a cross-border CPA (non-negotiable): the multi-member partnership return (Form 1065 + K-1 + K-2/K-3) and the ECI determination. Favorable nuance to confirm: since you both work physically in Pakistan, income is likely foreign-sourced → probably no US federal income tax and no §1446 withholding, even with US clients. Budget ~$800–2,000/yr. Good news: BOI/FinCEN reporting was removed for US-formed LLCs in 2025 - a WY LLC owes none.
