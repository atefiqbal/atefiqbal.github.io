The common enemy

The Frankenstack. A $500K-$5M brand ends up running five disconnected vendors, the Amazon guy, the Meta girl, an SEO retainer, an email freelancer, a creative shop, who never share data. Each optimizes their silo and reports their own inflated win. The founder becomes the unpaid integration layer, stitching five dashboards on a Sunday night while blended profit flatlines. The only teams that run these as one system are enterprise agencies that will not answer under $15K/mo. The enemy has two heads: the Frankenstack that fragments growth, and the enterprise agencies that gatekeep the fix.

The mechanism: The Agentic Growth Loop

Search feeds creative feeds the page feeds email feeds bidding, then closes and restarts. Every day.

Why a six-person team ships like twenty-five: agentic AI orchestration (Claude skills, MCP pipelines, automated harvesting and reporting) does the grunt work that eats a normal agency's headcount, so the humans spend their hours on judgment and taste. That is the honest reason Apticove can charge a fraction of enterprise pricing without cutting corners. The AI is in the mechanism, never the headline, because in 2026 "AI-powered" reads as slop. We just ship like it.

3 The complete catalog

Seven service families

The front door is deliberately narrow. The back room does all of this.

The search stack, defined in four layers (the piece almost no competitor audits in 2026):
  • SEO - Search Engine Optimization. Crawlability, Core Web Vitals, keyword-family targeting. Your pages get found, indexed, ranked. Table stakes.
  • SXO - Search Experience Optimization. Merges ranking with on-page experience so a page ranks and converts. The click is the starting line, not the finish.
  • AEO - Answer Engine Optimization. Become the cited answer in AI answer surfaces, snippets, and voice, via schema, LLMs.txt, and a third-party citation base.
  • GEO - Generative Engine Optimization. Get cited and recommended inside ChatGPT, Perplexity, Google AI Overviews, Gemini, and Amazon Rufus.

Say this to every prospect: ~60% of US consumers already use AI tools for some search, and only ~7.8% of Google's top-3 results get cited by ChatGPT. SEO earns the click; AEO and GEO decide whether a click is ever offered.

I · Amazon & Marketplaces

Amazon SEO, PPC (SP/SB/SD/SBV), DSP, A+/Storefront, catalog/variation, account health & compliance, private label, wholesale/FBA, online arbitrage, plus Walmart, eBay, Etsy. Full P&L ownership of the channel.

II · The Search Stack

SEO + SXO + AEO + GEO run as one program (see above). The first two win the human; the last two win the machine.

III · Conversion (CRO)

CRO, PDP optimization, landing pages, funnel/checkout de-friction, hypothesis-first A/B testing. The cheapest growth is the traffic you already paid for.

IV · Creative

AI creative direction, static ads, video/UGC, product photography, A+/listing imagery, structured creative testing. AI is the engine; taste is the product.

V · Paid Media

Meta, TikTok, Google/PMax, Pinterest, retargeting, omnichannel orchestration measured on blended MER, not siloed platform ROAS.

VI · Lifecycle & Retention

Email campaigns, Klaviyo flows, SMS, RFM segmentation, newsletters. Acquisition is rented; retention is owned.

VII · Strategy & Diagnostics

Growth audits, full-funnel strategy, and the flagship $1,500 Growth Teardown. Diagnostic before prescriptive: find the true constraint before anyone touches a campaign.
