Build principles

  • Bidding: Discovery = Down Only; Exact/Single-Keyword/Harvest = Up & Down : proven intent gets dynamic upside, discovery rides Down Only.
  • Placement: Top-of-Search 50-80% on Exact/Single-Keyword/Harvest; Product Targeting & Auto Subs/Comps push Product Page +40%; Catch-All gets no modifiers.
  • Negation: lean : brand phrase + single-keyword exact + theme exact used as negatives in Broad/Phrase; no negatives on Exact, Product Targeting, Auto, or Catch-All.
  • Brand terms (heartland antlers / heartlandantlers / heartland) used as phrase-negatives across Broad+Phrase and as positives in Brand-Only and Brand+Keyword campaigns.
  • Cannibalization filter: keywords excluded when that (keyword, match type) is already active AND converting in a live campaign (>=2 clicks threshold); result 0 duplicates.
  • Daily-budget tier multipliers: hero 1.5x, core 1.0x, defense 0.7x, untapped 1.2x applied to base per-block budget.
  • Single-Keyword Exact: one proven keyword per campaign for maximum bid and budget control; Top-of-Search 50%.
  • Stacked Exact: multiple proven exact terms grouped, Top-of-Search 35%, Up & Down bidding on proven intent.
  • Modified broad match built with '+word +word' syntax at Down Only for restrained discovery, Top-of-Search 20%.
  • Product Targeting Defense: own-catalog ASINs targeted to defend detail pages, +15-40% Product Page modifier.
  • Harvest campaigns isolate search terms that already converted, promoted into their own exact-match campaign.
  • Auto Loose + Auto Close split so loose-match discovery and near-match relevance carry separate bids and budgets.
